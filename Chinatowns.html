I have always loved visiting different Chinatowns around the world, as it has helped me understand other people and cultures better. Being a quarter Japanese, this has been a small way I have been able to connect with my Asian heritage. Below, I share some of my favorite information, pictures, and videos about Chinatowns around the world. This is a mix of my own photography and other people's work. I hope you enjoy!
